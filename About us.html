Mission Statement

From the start, the Project No Rona team strives to examine the data collected over the span of several months and present a factual and detailed report to The Most Honourable Andrew Holness, highlighting the impact of the pandemic on Jamaica.

Project No Rona Team

The Project No Rona team consist of five dedicated Jamaicans who aid in providing reliable and accurate information on COVID-19 to wherever needed.
